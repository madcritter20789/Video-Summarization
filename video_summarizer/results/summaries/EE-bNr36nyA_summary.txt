**General**:
1. As extension educators, we get frequent phone calls concerning paper wasps and their nests.
2. Paper wasps are actually a beneficial insect.
3. They're out collecting caterpillars off of your crops.
4. When you get too close to them, they're indiscriminate stingers.
5. The University of Wyoming Extension has a new way to remove wasps from the eaves of your home.
6. Use a spray to kill the wasps and then clean off the nest.
7. This is Jeff Edwards from the ground up.
8. For more information, visit wyomingextension.org..
